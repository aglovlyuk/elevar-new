Elevar Quick Draw

Elevar Quick Draw is a subsidiary of Elevar Design Group, a powerful architecture and engineering firm with offices in Cincinnati, Dayton, Lexington, and Cleveland. Under one roof, we employ architects, engineers, interior designers, planners, and support staff for design projects in a wide variety of markets. Quick Draw works hand-in-hand with the resources afforded by Elevar Design Group to expedite the draw process for our lending partners. With Quick Draw your institution receives timely information necessary to verify work so you can ensure your loans remain in balance.

We understand that the most critical part of the construction draw process is the quick turn-around of the draw report for each phase of a project. The longer this process takes, the more risk you and your partners attract. Your institution practices under a high degree of due diligence to ensure your risk is mitigated. That is why it is critical that the draw process is completed in a timely and efficient manner.

Elevar Quick Draw is a team of professionals with decades of experience in the design and construction industry. We work with financial institutions to ease the burden of the construction draw process. Using custom software to generate reports, you will receive your construction draw report ahead of plan.

Why Elevar Quickdraw?

Elevar Quick Draw is a locally-owned subsidiary of Elevar Design Group, the largest minority-owned design firm in Cincinnati, OH. Operated by a Client Manager and Executive Team with more than 90 combined years of experience, we provide quality services unmatched by other firms.

Our software generates all-encompassing reports in one complete document to help ensure the construction draw process is done as thoroughly and efficiently as possible. Elevar Quick Draw maintains all file backups, reducing the need for you to keep track of a paper trail.

It is simple: You lend. They build. We monitor. Your money is kept in balance with construction progress to aid in an easy, fast financial loan transfer.
